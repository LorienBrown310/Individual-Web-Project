About Me

Growing up with two parents in IT, I always had an interest in technology and problem-solving. From an early age, I was fascinated by the way computers and software could be used to create solutions and improve everyday life. This passion naturally led me to pursue a degree in Information Technology at Kennesaw State University, where I could learn more about the world of technology.

As a junior at KSU, I have focused on developing practical experience through coursework and working as a co-op. One of the most rewarding experiences has been my co-op at McKenney's, where, not only have I enhanced my abilities in areas such as troubleshooting and technical problem-solving, but it has also taught me the importance of teamwork and effective communication. Additionally, my coursework has provided a solid foundation in various IT fields, including software development, networking, etc..., further preparing me for a career in the tech industry.

Looking ahead, I am excited to continue my journey in the tech industry with innovative companies that are pushing the boundaries of technology. I am particularly interested in artificial intelligence for Data Analytics and Technology. Overall, I hope to use my skills and knowledge to create impactful solutions that address real-world challenges and improve the quality of life for everyone.
